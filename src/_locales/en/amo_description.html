Cast Kodi, as the name suggests, adds the ability to cast your videos and music to Kodi:
  • videos: direct links (avi, mkv, ...), YouTube, Twitch, Vimeo, Facebook, Dailymotion, PeerTube, Rutube, CollegeHumor, Liveleak, DevTube, AlloCin√©, JeuxVideoCom, Air Mozilla and Dumpert;
  • music: direct links (mp3, flac, ...), SoundCloud, Mixcloud and Arte Radio.
Three items can be added on context menu of link / page / selection of videos or audio : Play now to Kodi, Play next to Kodi and Queue item to Kodi. A remote (accessible with toolbar button) is also present to cast current tab to Kodi and to : pause, go to next element, adjust the volume, ... In Kodi, you must Allow remote control via HTTP. This option is on Settings / Services / Control. Then you must configure Cast Kodi Options by copying values filled in Kodi: Port and possibly Username and Password. You must provide an IP Address of the server hosting Kodi; this information is displayed on Kodi's Settings / System info / Summary page. To play videos/music hosted on specific platforms, Kodi plugins such as YouTube, Twitch, Vimeo, DailyMotion.com, CollegeHumor, Dumpert, SoundCloud or Mixcloud must be installed.

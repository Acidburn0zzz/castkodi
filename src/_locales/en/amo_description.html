Cast Kodi, as the name suggests, adds the ability to cast your videos and music to Kodi:
  • videos: direct links (avi, mkv, ...), YouTube1, Twitch1, Vimeo1, Instagram, torrents1, magnets1 and Ace Stream1, AlloCiné, BitChute, Dailymotion1, DevTube, Dumpert1, Facebook, Flickr, Full30, JeuxVideoCom, LiveLeak, PeerTube, Pippa, Первый канал, Rutube, Steam, StormoTV, Útvarp Saga, ...
  • music: direct links (mp3, flac, ...), SoundCloud1 and Apple Podcasts, Arte Radio, Mixcloud1, My Cloud Player, podCloud, Radioline, ...
Cast Kodi also analyzes the pages for videos, music or external platforms integrations. For example, if a page displays a YouTube video, this video will be sent to Kodi. To cast videos / music, three items can be added on context menu of link / page / selection of videos or audio : Play now to Kodi, Play next to Kodi and Queue item to Kodi. A remote (accessible with toolbar button) is also present to cast current tab to Kodi and to : pause, go to next element, adjust the volume, ... In Kodi, you must Allow remote control from applications on other systems. This option is on Settings / Services / Control. Then you must configure Cast Kodi Options by copying IP address of the server hosting Kodi; this information is displayed on Kodi's Settings / System information / Summary page. 1 To play videos/music hosted on this platforms, Kodi plugins such as YouTube, Twitch, Vimeo, Elementum (torrents/magnets), Plexus (Ace Stream), DailyMotion.com, Dumpert, SoundCloud or Mixcloud must be installed.

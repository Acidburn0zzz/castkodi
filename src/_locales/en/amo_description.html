Cast Kodi, as the name suggests, adds the ability to cast your videos and music to Kodi:
  • videos: direct links (avi, mkv, ...), YouTube, Twitch, Vimeo, Instagram, torrents, magnets and Ace Stream, AlloCiné, Dailymotion, DevTube, Dumpert, Facebook, Flickr, Full30, JeuxVideoCom, PeerTube, Pippa, Rutube, StormoTV, ...
  • music: direct links (mp3, flac, ...), SoundCloud and Apple Podcasts, Arte Radio, Mixcloud, Radioline, ...
Cast Kodi also analyzes the pages for videos, music or external platforms integrations. For example, if a page displays a YouTube video, this video will be sent to Kodi. The following sites (and probably others) are supported: LiveLeak, Útvarp Saga, ... To cast videos / music, three items can be added on context menu of link / page / selection of videos or audio : Play now to Kodi, Play next to Kodi and Queue item to Kodi. A remote (accessible with toolbar button) is also present to cast current tab to Kodi and to : pause, go to next element, adjust the volume, ... In Kodi, you must Allow remote control from applications on other systems. This option is on Settings / Services / Control. Then you must configure Cast Kodi Options by copying IP address of the server hosting Kodi; this information is displayed on Kodi's Settings / System information / Summary page. To play videos/music hosted on specific platforms, Kodi plugins such as YouTube, Twitch, Vimeo, Elementum (torrents/magnets), Plexus (Ace Stream), DailyMotion.com, Dumpert, SoundCloud or Mixcloud must be installed.

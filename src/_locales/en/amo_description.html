Cast Kodi, as the name suggests, adds the ability to cast your videos and music to Kodi:
  • videos: direct links (avi, mkv, ...), YouTube, Facebook, Twitch, Vimeo, Dailymotion, CollegeHumor, Air Mozilla and Dumpert;
  • music: direct links (mp3, flac, ...), SoundCloud and Mixcloud.
A new item "Cast to Kodi" is added on context menu of link / page / selection of videos or audio. There, two choices are available: Play or Queue Item. In Kodi, you must Allow remote control via HTTP. This option is on Settings / Services / Control. Then you must configure Cast Kodi Options by copying values filled in Kodi: Port and possibly Username and Password. You must provide an IP Address of the server hosting Kodi; this information is displayed on Kodi's Settings / System info / Summary page. To play videos/music hosted on specific platforms, Kodi plugins such as YouTube, Twitch, Vimeo, DailyMotion.com, CollegeHumor, Dumpert, SoundCloud or Mixcloud must be installed.
